Project Details

User research and UI Design for Phyto Tech App

Phyto-Tech is an app mainly designed for plant disease detection and in addition to that it helps in distinguishing plants from weeds. A chatbot system is created to solve doubts related farming as well as usage of our app. For further support we have a system to contact Agricultural Specialist. The technique used here is Deep Learning for disease and weed detection.

I have developed the UI based on intensive user research and surveys. Standard font and color system is followed tailored according to the objective of the app.

Web Application Design - Poultry Aid System

Poultry farming has great marketing potential in Kerala But farmers face difficulty in reaching self -sufficiency due to lack of quality feeds,unprecedented price hike, artificial scarcity of resources etc. Thus there is need for system that help in dealing with requirements of poultry farms. This application that helps poultry farmers to keep track of feeding shops and their requirements aiding in building a better environment for the growth of farmers. The Poultry Farmer Aiding System is mainly based on poultry feed supply chain monitoring. Blockchain a decentralized distributed ledger shall be used to enable a more accurate and end to end tracking of supply chain. Moreover system shall help identify quality services and shall have details of poultry farms and centres.
